Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Stackd ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Stackd mobile application and website (the "Service"). This page explains our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Information We Collect

Stackd does not require you to create an account. When you use the Service, the following data is processed:

Photos: Images you upload are sent to our server for AI analysis. Photos are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers after the scan is complete.

Usage data: We may collect anonymous usage information such as scan frequency and app performance data to improve the Service.

How We Use Your Information

Photos you submit are used solely to identify items and provide resale price estimates. We do not store, share, or sell your photos or any personal information to third parties.

Third Party Services

Stackd uses the following third party services:

Anthropic Claude API: Used for AI image analysis. Images are processed according to Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

eBay Browse API: Used to retrieve current market pricing data. No personal data is shared with eBay.

Google AdSense: Used to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visit. You can opt out at google.com/settings/ads.

Data Retention

We do not retain photos or personal data after your scan is complete. Anonymous usage analytics may be retained for up to 90 days.

Children's Privacy

Stackd is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
